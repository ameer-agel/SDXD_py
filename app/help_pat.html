Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update or edit an existing patient profile?

Navigate to 'Records' from the sidebar, search for the patient's name, click on their profile, and then select 'Edit Details' to update their information or treatment history.

What is the diagnostic accuracy of the AI system?

The SDXD system operates at up to 98% accuracy in detecting caries and root anomalies. However, results should always be reviewed and approved by the practitioner.
